The two competitions Scoring Lock times FAQ
Original Predictor

Pick before a ball is kicked

  • Predict the score in every group match, any time before it locks.
  • Arm up to 5 jokers on the group scores you're most confident about — jokers double your points.
  • Pick who advances through your own permanent bracket, all the way to the champion. Winner picks only — no scores, no jokers here.
  • Everything locks the moment the opening match kicks off.
KO Predictor

A second game once the real knockouts are set

  • Opens once the real Round of 16 fixtures are known — separate from your Original bracket picks.
  • Predict the score, the advancing team and how the match is decided for each real fixture.
  • Its own 5 jokers, its own leaderboard.
  • Never mixes with your Original Predictor points — two competitions, two totals.

Common questions

FAQ

Are the Original Predictor and KO Predictor points combined?

No — two separate competitions, two separate leaderboards. Your Original Predictor score is fixed at kick-off; your KO Predictor score builds up separately once the real knockouts begin.

Can I change a prediction after I save it?

Yes, any time up until it locks. Group scores and bracket picks all lock together at the tournament's first kick-off.

What do jokers actually do?

Arming a joker doubles the points from that one prediction if it comes in correct — and costs you nothing if it doesn't. You get 5 to use across your group predictions, and 5 more, entirely separately, for the KO Predictor. There are no jokers on the bracket — it's winner-picks only.

What happens if I don't use all my jokers?

Nothing — there's no penalty. Unused jokers just don't add anything, so there's no reason to hold them back.

What if my bracket pick no longer matches my group predictions?

If you change a group prediction after already picking a bracket winner that depended on it, that bracket tie gets flagged for a re-pick. Everything else in your bracket that's unaffected stays exactly as you left it.
